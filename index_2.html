About Me

Welcome to my world, where data and detailed analysis meet strategic business thinking. With a solid educational foundation from Purdue University's Daniels School of Business, I've sharpened my skills in turning complex problems into clear, actionable insights. My career is built on a bedrock of delivering results, managing cross-functional teams, and leading projects that push the envelope.

Specializing in Python, SQL, and R, I tackle data with a toolkit that includes PySpark, Pandas, and Scikit-learn, among other technologies. Whether it's through advanced analytics or building efficient data pipelines in the cloud, my goal is always to create value and drive progress. My work as a Project Manager has led me to innovate forecasting methods, improving efficiency and significantly reducing manual work.

But life isn't all numbers and charts. Outside the office, I'm an avid traveler, reader, history enthusiast, and formula one fan.

I am here to connect the dots, to transform raw data into strategies that work, and to move businesses forward. If you're looking for a partner in navigating the data-driven challenges of today's world, let's get in touch.
